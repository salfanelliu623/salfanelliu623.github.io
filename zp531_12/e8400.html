【本の内容】
Display Art features examples of visual merchandising and window design by art directors graphic and window designers across the globe in an impressive selection that showcases sophisticated projects and also simple minimal and highly creative window displays.
The works included in this volume offer successful ideas concerning aspects such as lighting design structures custom lettering amusing illustrations delicate paper crafting textures or installations made of multi-materials such as wood steel fabric or rubber .
In all cases they have resulted in alluring artwork that appeals to the senses - and instincts - of consumers and passers-by. This book is the perfect inspirational guide for art directors visual merchandising designers and fashion professionals.
