[For PDW Class Shorty M4] Lightweight short RAS with boldly cut upper rails. The mounting base is a type that secures the RAS with a hex bolt to a barrel nut like NOVESKE NRS. QD type sling swivels on the bottom and sides for attachment Both rails can be mounted 2 at a time so you can set it to your preferred position.
