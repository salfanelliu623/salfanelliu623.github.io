But now Monocle raises the bar: how do you run a great country? They are not talking about might muscle or nationalism-although you need a bit of a swagger every now and then. How to Make a Nation: A Monocle Guide is a thought-provoking primer that informs and inspires. The best ideas don"t need to be invented-they need to be found and copied.
