Bluetooth Pairing: 

A. Single Speaker Mode: 

1. Turn on the speaker by long pressing the power button.

2. Turn on the Bluetooth of mobile phone, Search for the Bluetooth pairing name "VCB3D", and Connect.

B. Stereo Mode: 

1. Turn on the two speakers separately by Long pressing power button. 

2. Double press the power button on one of the speakers, and the two speakers will pair with each other after 2 seconds. After the prompt tone "Dong", it is successfully connected. The the speaker with flashing blue indicator as the "MASTER UNIT" and the other one with the stable blue indicator as the "SLAVE UNIT". After the first-time pairing is successful, it will be automatically connected when you turn it on next time. 

3. Turn on the Bluetooth of mobile phone, Search for the Bluetooth pairing name "VCB3D" and click pairing. The Bluetooth is connected after hearing the prompt tone. Then you can play the sound source with your player.

*If re-use fails to automatically pair or connect, delete the original "VCB3D" device and restart the search for a new device "Vcb3d" and connect.

*The mobile phone and speaker can only be connected by one- to-one. If you need to connect the second mobile phone for use, please turn off the Bluetooth of the first mobile phone first.

Nomal Calling: 

- Incoming call status, click the power button to answer the call.

- During answering status, click the power key to hang up the call.

- Answer the call and towards to the audio microphone.

Lower Battery Indicator: When the power is less than or equal to 10%, a warning tone will be given. The low power prompt tone will be again after 10 minutes until the speaker is off
